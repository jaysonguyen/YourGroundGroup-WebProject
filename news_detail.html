Couples when coming to Da Lat, want to find themselves a comfortable, poetic space, can visit these 4 extremely chill homestays.
With poetic scenery, Da Lat city (Lam Dong) is always a famous destination visited by many tourists, especially couples who choose to travel and enjoy their honeymoon. This romantic city always has enough accommodation services for you to choose from.

Photo: Memory homestay Dalat

If couples want to find themselves a romantic and quiet space to visit and explore the wonderful beauty of Da Lat, you can visit the 4 “extremely chill” homestays below.

1. Dalat Memories Homestay

Dalat Memories Homestay has a spacious garden garden designed in a classical style, which is both close to the natural landscape and gives you a quiet, rustic and comfortable space.

Photo: Memory homestay Dalat

A plus point for Memory homestay Dalat is that it has a beautiful view, and saves money through the fact that the cost of the room includes a breakfast, visitors can also drink coffee and admire the romantic scenery of Da Lat. full of poetic completely free.

Photo: Memory homestay Dalat

2. Waiting for a Homestay

About 5 km from Da Lat city center, Doi Mot Nguoi homestay is a peaceful little corner where the pine forest is green, so it is very suitable for many couples to come to Da Lat to enjoy peace and warmth.

Photo: Halo Travel

In particular, this homestay was built by a group of young people who love mountains and forests, passionate about the beautiful natural landscape of Da Lat. Therefore, these young people better understand what customers want, especially what young couples need when coming here.

3. Dalat Teepee Homestay

Dalat Teepee homestay is one of many lucky people who owns the most beautiful and charming location in Da Lat city. Around Dalat Teepee is a large natural scene, surrounded by green trees, with fresh and cool air.

Photo: Halo Travel

In particular, this homestay was built by a group of young people who love mountains and forests, passionate about the beautiful natural landscape of Da Lat. Therefore, these young people better understand what customers want, especially what young couples need when coming here.

4. The Kupid Homestay

Photo: The Kupid homestay

The Kupid homestay has a simple, cozy design, evoking the feeling of familiarity like ancient houses. The dominant style at this homestay has a harmonious combination of minimalist white tones with some extremely classic retro and vintage decorations.
